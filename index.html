Who am I?

Ever since I was young, reading and writing were always my passion. When I was in my teens and fully realized that I could not be happy if I did not read and write forever, I wanted to study and work hard enough to make my passion my living. But even more than that, I wanted my passion to impact others.

After completing my Honours BA in English Literature at the University of Toronto, I pursued my English master's degree and my publishing certificate at Ryerson University, through which I was afforded numerous opportunities to teach, research, and demonstrate my editing, indexing, coding, and curriculum development skills. Recent experiences include Scholastic Canada, the United Nations, and Edinburgh University Press.

Today, I continue to pursue opportunities where I can use my impassioned abilities to make a difference. My goal is to use writing to empower the world.
